greenwichsrt(Jdate)

compute greenwich sidereal time from D. Vallado 4th edition

Inputs

Jdate: Julian days from Jan 1, 4713 BCE from juliantime(utc) or juliandate(utc)

Outputs

gst: greenwich sidereal time    [0, 2pi)
